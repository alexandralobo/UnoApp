Rules

  • At the start of the game, each player is dealt 7 cards, the remaining cards will be facedown to form a draw pile, and the top card of the draw pile will begin a discard pile. The first top card of the discard pile cannot be a Wild or Wild Draw 4, and the first player will be randomly selected.

  • There are 5 specials cards which are:
    • Draw 2 Card - When a player plays this card, the next person to play must draw 2 cards and forfeit his/her turn.
    • Reverse Card - This card reverses direction of play. Play to the left now passes to the right, and vice versa.
    • Skip Card - The next person in line to play after this card is played loses his/her turn and is "skipped".
    • Wild Card - When a player palays this card, the player may change the color being played to any color (including the current color) to continue play. A player may play a Wild card even if you have another playable card in hand.
    • Wild Draw 4 Card - This card allows the player to call the next color played and requires the next player to pick 4 cards from the draw pile and forfeit his/her turn.

  • Each player must match the top card on the discard pile either by number, color, or word. If the player does not have anything that matches, they must pick a card from the draw pile until the player gets a card that matches.

  • Before the player play his/her next to last card, the player must press "UNO". Otherwise, another play may catch the player with just one card, and the player that did not press UNO must pick FOUR more cards from the draw pile.
